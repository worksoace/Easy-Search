About

Easy Search

Easy Search is an offline Bible study app built for quick word lookup, reference lookup, Bible names, and Strong's concordance study without needing the internet.

Use the menu bar to change preferences, hide or restore sections, and open the built-in help screens. Your Bible position, theme, and layout are saved locally so the app can reopen where you left off.

Tutorial

How to use the app

Press Ctrl+K to open the search box, then type a word, phrase, or Bible reference. The search box stays hidden by default so the reader stays clean until you need it.

Right-click selected text anywhere in the dictionary, Bible names, concordance, or verses and choose Lookup to search it instantly. Right-click a section title bar and choose Close section to remove that panel from the screen.

Open the View menu to restore hidden sections. Use Show all sections when you want to bring everything back at once.

Bible navigation supports short book names such as Prov for Proverbs and Pro for Proverbs, plus verse ranges like John 3:16-18.

Press [ or ] to jump to the previous or next chapter. Use Alt+Left / Alt+Right to go back and forward through your navigation history.

Hover over any Strong's number chip (e.g. H430) to see a quick definition tooltip. Click it to open the full lexicon entry. Press C to copy the active verse to clipboard.

Press F to enter Focus mode — hides all panels except the Bible reader. Press F again or use the Focus button in the toolbar to exit.
